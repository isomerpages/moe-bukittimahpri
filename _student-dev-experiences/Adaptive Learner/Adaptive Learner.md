<p align="justify"><strong>Overview:</strong><br>
Adaptive Learners ask questions, explore possibilities, and persevere through challenges. They think critically, reflect on their learning, and adapt to different situations with creativity and confidence. By staying open-minded and flexible, they continue to grow and thrive in an ever-changing world. </p>
<p align="justify">
Through our <a style="onmouseover=" href="https://bukittimahpri.moe.edu.sg/level-signature-programmes/"><strong>Level Signature Programmes</strong></a>, <a style="onmouseover=" href="https://bukittimahpri.moe.edu.sg/interdisciplinary-project-work-ipw/"><strong>Interdisciplinary Project Work (IPW)</strong></a>, and the <a style="onmouseover=" href="https://bukittimahpri.moe.edu.sg/applied-learning-programme-alp/"><strong>Applied Learning Programme (ALP)</strong></a>, students engage in authentic tasks where they explore, collaborate, and apply learning beyond the classroom. These experiences build confidence, creativity and the mindset to thrive in a changing world. </p>
