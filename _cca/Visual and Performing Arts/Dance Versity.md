<p align="justify">
Dance-Versity&nbsp;is a vibrant CCA where students explore a variety of dance styles and express themselves creatively through movement. Through structured dance training and performance opportunities, students learn to be adaptable as they try new choreography, collaborate with different teammates, and respond confidently to challenges.
