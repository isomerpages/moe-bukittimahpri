<h4><strong>Art</strong></h4>
<p align="justify">In Art lessons, students are encouraged to explore ideas, express themselves, and make meaning through images and materials. Through engaging lessons and authentic learning experiences, they work with both traditional and digital art forms while developing creativity, visual literacy, and confidence.</p>
<p align="justify">
Students participate in inquiry-based discussions and collaborative learning, where they are encouraged to experiment with materials, share perspectives, and reflect on their work. Through creating and responding to art, students grow into curious thinkers, inventive creators, and thoughtful audiences who learn to view the world through new and imaginative lenses.</p>
